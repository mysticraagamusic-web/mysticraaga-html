હું શુદ્ધાત્મા છું

Hu Shudhatma Chu

MR-0005

🎵 Listen Now

About the Song

A meditative affirmation reminding listeners that their true identity is the eternal Pure Soul rather than the changing body, mind or emotions.

Meaning

The song contrasts the changing world with the unchanging Self, inviting deep self-realization and inner peace.
