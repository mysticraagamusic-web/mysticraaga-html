About the Song

Shahon Ka Shah is inspired by the famous doha of Sant Kabir: "चाह गई चिंता मिटी, मनुवा बेपरवाह। जिनको कछुन चाहिए, वो शाहों का शाह॥" The song celebrates the freedom that comes when desires disappear. Through a Sufi-Qawwali musical style, Mystic Raaga presents this timeless teaching for modern listeners.

Kabir's Doha

चाह गई चिंता मिटी,
मनुवा बेपरवाह।

जिनको कछुन चाहिए,
वो शाहों का शाह॥

Meaning

Kabir teaches that the richest person is not the one who owns everything, but the one who needs nothing. When craving ends, anxiety ends. True peace comes from inner completeness rather than external possessions.

FAQ

Who wrote this song?

The song is inspired by the teachings of Sant Kabir and presented by Mystic Raaga.

What does "Shahon Ka Shah" mean?

It means "The King of Kings"—the one who desires nothing and is therefore truly free.
