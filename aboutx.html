Official About Statement
Mystic Raaga is a premium spiritual music label dedicated to creating original music inspired by timeless wisdom found in sacred texts and the teachings of enlightened masters from diverse spiritual traditions.
Every composition is crafted with the intention of bringing listeners closer to inner peace, devotion, self-discovery, and ultimately, self-realization.
We believe that while paths may differ, the essence of spiritual wisdom is universal—and music is one of its most powerful expressions.

Vision
To become the world's most trusted home for timeless Indian spiritual music.

Mission
To create original music inspired by timeless spiritual wisdom that helps people experience greater peace, devotion, self-discovery, and self-realization.

Purpose
Our goal is not merely to entertain.
Our purpose is to awaken, inspire, and elevate.
Every song should leave the listener more peaceful, more aware, and a little closer to their true Self than before they pressed play.

Brand Philosophy
Music is one of humanity's oldest and most powerful spiritual languages.
Mystic Raaga believes that timeless wisdom exists across many spiritual traditions. When expressed through beautiful music, these teachings become easier to experience, remember, and live.
Our music is inspired by wisdom—not limited by religious boundaries. While paths may differ, the essence of spiritual truth remains universal.

Core Values

  • Authenticity - We create music rooted in genuine spiritual teachings and timeless wisdom.
  • Peace - Every composition should create calmness and inner stillness.
  • Devotion - Music should inspire humility, gratitude, surrender, and love for the Divine.
  • Self-Discovery - Songs should encourage reflection and help listeners understand themselves more deeply.
  • Excellence - Every lyric, melody, arrangement, and visual should meet high artistic standards.
  • Timelessness - We create music intended to remain meaningful for decades.
  • Inclusiveness - We respectfully draw inspiration from diverse spiritual traditions while honoring the authenticity of each.
