परस्त्री मातेसमान

Parstri Matesaman

MR-0006

🎵 Listen Now

About the Song

A musical tribute inspired by the values associated with Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj, emphasizing character, restraint and respect for women.

Meaning

The song teaches that true bravery is reflected not only in courage but also in dignity, honour and everyday conduct.
