About the Song

મોક્ષમાળા બોલે છે imagines the revered spiritual classic Mokshmala speaking directly to the seeker. Rather than being just a book, it becomes a compassionate friend that gently inspires wisdom, self-reflection, virtues, and inner awakening.

Meaning

The song reminds us that true knowledge is meant to transform life. Mokshmala lovingly encourages the reader to seek truth, ask questions, cultivate compassion, and share wisdom with others. Every verse points toward lighting the lamp of the Self.
