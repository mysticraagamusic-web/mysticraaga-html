About the Song

હું અને તમે (Hu Ane Tame) is a heartfelt Gujarati devotional prayer expressing a seeker's longing to experience the constant presence of Simandhar Swami. Every verse is a conversation of love, surrender, trust and companionship with the Divine.

Meaning

The deepest prayer is not asking for more—it is asking the Divine to remain with us in every step of life.

This song beautifully portrays devotion through simple yet profound expressions: walk with me when I move, strengthen me when I fall, guide me when I lose my way, and dwell forever in the temple of my heart.
